Veggie Supreme Pizza

Description

A vibrant and delicious pizza packed with colorful veggies, mozzarella, and a touch of Italian seasoning. A great choice for vegetarians or anyone looking for a healthy, flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (pizza sauce or marinara)
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • Fresh spinach leaves (about 1/2 cup)
  • Olive oil (for drizzling)
  • Italian seasoning, to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a pizza stone in the oven if using.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to about 12 inches. Place it on parchment paper if using a pizza stone.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese, spreading it evenly across the pizza.
  5. Arrange the sliced red onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, black olives, and spinach leaves on top of the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle a pinch of Italian seasoning and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Drizzle with olive oil before serving.
  9. Slice, serve, and enjoy your veggie-packed pizza!
