Simple Margherita Pizza

Description

A classic, easy-to-make Margherita pizza with a crispy, golden crust, topped with fresh mozzarella, tangy tomato sauce, and fragrant basil leaves. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a casual gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (preferably pizza sauce or crushed tomatoes)
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves (about 6-8)
  • Olive oil (for drizzling)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up as well.
  2. Prepare the Dough: Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness (about 12 inches for a medium pizza). Transfer to a parchment paper if using a pizza stone.
  3. Add Sauce: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Top with Cheese: Arrange slices of fresh mozzarella evenly over the sauce.
  5. Bake the Pizza: Transfer the pizza to the oven (on the stone or a baking sheet) and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned.
  6. Finish with Basil and Olive Oil: Once out of the oven, scatter fresh basil leaves over the top, and drizzle with a little olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Slice and Serve: Slice the pizza and enjoy!
