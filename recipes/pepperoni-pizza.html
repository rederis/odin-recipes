Pepperoni Pizza

Description

A crowd-pleasing classic with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, gooey melted mozzarella, and plenty of savory pepperoni. A timeless favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (pizza sauce or marinara)
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • Olive oil (for drizzling)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness (about 12 inches). Transfer to parchment paper if using a stone.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella evenly over the sauce.
  5. Top with sliced pepperoni, ensuring even coverage across the pizza.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned.
  7. Once out of the oven, drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Slice, serve, and enjoy!
